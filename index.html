About Expert Dry Cleaners

Welcome to Expert Dry Cleaners, where we combine traditional care with modern techniques to deliver unparalleled garment cleaning. With over 20 years of experience, we are dedicated to preserving the quality and extending the life of your cherished clothing.

Our team of skilled professionals uses eco-friendly solvents and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure every item, from delicate silks to heavy draperies, receives the meticulous attention it deserves. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Our Services

Dry Cleaning

Expert dry cleaning for all types of garments, including suits, dresses, and delicate fabrics. We use gentle, effective methods.

Commercial Laundry Services

Scalable, professional-grade cleaning solutions tailored for hotels, hospitals, gyms, and businesses that demand freshness at volume.

Alterations & Repairs

From minor repairs to custom alterations, our skilled tailors ensure your clothes fit perfectly and look their best.

Shoe Laundry Service

A professional cleaning solution that restores your favorite footwear to its fresh, spotless glory—one sole at a time.

Wash & Iron

Careful and specialized cleaning for leather and suede garments, restoring their look and feel.

Wash & Fold

Hassle-free laundry service that cleans, dries, and neatly folds your clothes—ready to wear or store.
